George Brown College, located in Toronto, Ontario, is a leading Canadian public college established in 1967.  
Named after George Brown, a notable politician and journalist, the college offers programs designed for career readiness.

Programs include diplomas, degrees, and postgraduate certificates in fields like business, technology, healthcare, hospitality, and community services.  

The college operates three main campuses:
- St. James Campus
- Casa Loma Campus
- Waterfront Campus
George Brown emphasizes hands-on learning through labs, co-op programs, and real-world projects.  

Students benefit from strong partnerships with industry leaders, and the college provides support services such as career guidance, academic advising, and workshops.  

Innovation, diversity, and practical skills development are key focuses of George Brown College.
